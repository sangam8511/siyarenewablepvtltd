Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the quote?

The quote provides an estimate based on your electricity consumption and location. Our engineers will conduct a site assessment for a more precise estimate.

How long does installation take?

Installation timeline depends on system size. Typically, residential systems take 1-2 weeks, while commercial systems can range from 2-8 weeks.

Do I need maintenance?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend periodic cleaning and annual inspections to ensure optimal performance.

What's the warranty coverage?

We provide 25-year panel warranty, 10-year inverter warranty, and 5-year workmanship warranty covering all installation and equipment defects.
